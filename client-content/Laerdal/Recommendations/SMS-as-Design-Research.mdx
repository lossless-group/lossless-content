# SMS as Design Research

## Overview
Get more valuable insights with greater frequency by using Sales, Marketing, and Service (SMS) as a [[Design Research]] Operation.

## Key Points
- Leverage existing customer touchpoints for research
- Integrate research into daily operations
- Scale insights collection

## Implementation
1. Identify key SMS touchpoints
2. Design research questions
3. Implement feedback loops
4. Analyze and act on insights

## Benefits
- Real-time customer feedback
- Higher response rates
- More natural customer interactions
- Cost-effective research method
