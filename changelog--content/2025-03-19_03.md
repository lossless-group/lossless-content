### YAML Tag Validation Pattern
```javascript
/(?:tags:\s*(?:\[.*?\]|.*?,.*?|['"].*?['"])|(?:^|\n)\s*-\s*\w+[^\S\n]+\w+)/
```

Detects and corrects:
- Array syntax: `tags: ["tag1", "tag2"]`
- Comma separation: `tags: tag1, tag2`
- Quoted tags: `tags: 'tag1'` or `"tag2"`
- Space-separated words: `tags:\n- Tag With Spaces`
