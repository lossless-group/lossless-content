Scientific studies indicate that humans process visual information up to 60,000 times faster than text, with an estimated 90% of information transmitted to brains being visual. [^n2b1p9] This phenomenon, known as the picture superiority effect, explains why AI-generated visuals can be so persuasive and why misinformation presented in convincing visual formats spreads so effectively. [^n2b1p9]
