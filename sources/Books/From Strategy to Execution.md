# Why It Matters to Innovators

- Provides actionable diagnostics for why 70-90% of strategies fail in execution, emphasizing coordination over incentives or leadership alone.[8][7]
- Teaches frameworks like "from firefighting to flow" and "infrastructure of trust" to diagnose execution barriers and install mental models for momentum-building.[8]
- Illuminates operational innovation by framing execution as two organizational layers—strategy and delivery—requiring granular initiatives and resource reallocation.[8][2]
- Connects to [[concepts/Objectives & Key Results]] via priority-setting and progress tracking, helping innovators adapt assumptions and launch next S-curves.[2][8]
- Counters hallucinated visions (per Edison quote) with "only valid strategy is one that can be executed," installing execution-first mental models.[7]
