# Peter Diamandis
![Professional headshot of Peter H. Diamandis in business attire, smiling against a neutral background](https://d28hgpri8am2if.cloudfront.net/author_images/21708259/peter-h-diamandis-77247725.jpg)

_*Peter Diamandis is a high-energy futurist entrepreneur best known for turning bold space and longevity "moonshots" into structured innovation platforms like XPRIZE and Singularity University.*_

Peter H. Diamandis is a person: an American engineer, physician, and serial entrepreneur active since the 1980s, whose public presence today centers on innovation, exponential technologies, and human longevity. [^n8bmqm] [^ygo8nj] [^npg1p7] He founded and serves as Executive Chairman of the XPRIZE Foundation and is the Executive Founder of Singularity University, both of which shaped the modern incentive-prize and exponential-tech ecosystems. [^n8bmqm] [^ygo8nj] [^87shnb] He was born in 1961 in New York City to Greek parents, trained at MIT and Harvard Medical School, and has started or co-founded over 25 companies across health-tech, space, education, and venture capital. [^n8bmqm] [^n9hx8k] [^ygo8nj] [^npg1p7] [^ecrl5z] Innovation consultants return to him for a consistent, optimistic framework on leveraging exponential technologies to solve grand challenges and create "a world of abundance."[^ygo8nj] [^npg1p7]

## The People Behind It

(Section adapted for a person: this is Peter Diamandis himself.)

- Peter H. Diamandis was born on May 20, 1961, in New York City/the Bronx to Greek parents and developed an early passion for space and education. [^n8bmqm] [^n9hx8k] [^ecrl5z]
- He earned degrees in molecular genetics and aerospace engineering (aeronautics and astronautics) from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology, and later an M.D. from Harvard Medical School, though he chose entrepreneurship over clinical practice. [^n8bmqm] [^n9hx8k] [^ygo8nj]
- He is best known as the Founder and Executive Chairman of the XPRIZE Foundation, which "leads the world in designing and operating large-scale incentive competitions" to solve global challenges. [^n8bmqm] [^ygo8nj] [^87shnb]
- He is also the Executive Founder of Singularity University, a "global learning and innovation community using exponential technologies to tackle the world’s biggest challenges."[^n8bmqm] [^ygo8nj] [^87shnb]
- As an entrepreneur, he has founded or co‑founded more than 25 companies in sectors including health-tech, longevity, commercial spaceflight, venture capital, and education—among them Zero Gravity Corporation, Space Adventures, Rocket Racing League, International Space University, Planetary Resources, Celularity, Vaxxinity, Fountain Life, and BOLD Capital Partners. [^n8bmqm] [^n9hx8k] [^ygo8nj] [^npg1p7] [^3d4629]
- Diamandis is a New York Times bestselling author of multiple books, including *Abundance: The Future Is Better Than You Think*, *Bold: How to Go Big, Create Wealth and Impact the World*, *The Future Is Faster Than You Think*, and, most recently, *Life Force* (co-authored with Tony Robbins). [^n9hx8k] [^ygo8nj] [^npg1p7]

## Catalog of Notable Works

(As a person, his "catalog" is his books, initiatives, and signature content.)

- [Abundance: The Future Is Better Than You Think](https://www.diamandis.com/books) — 2012 — Co‑authored with Steven Kotler, this bestseller argues that exponential technologies, DIY innovators, and rising billions can help humanity move from scarcity to abundance. [^n9hx8k] [^ygo8nj]
- [Bold: How to Go Big, Create Wealth and Impact the World](https://www.diamandis.com/books) — 2015 — A playbook for entrepreneurs on using exponential technologies, crowdsourcing, and incentive prizes to scale impact and build massively transformative companies. [^ygo8nj]
- [The Future Is Faster Than You Think](https://www.diamandis.com/books) — 2020 — Explores how converging technologies (AI, robotics, VR/AR, digital biology, etc.) will rapidly transform industries over the next decade. [^ygo8nj]
- [Life Force](https://www.diamandis.com/books) — 2022 — Co‑authored with Tony Robbins, this book surveys cutting‑edge advances in regenerative medicine, diagnostics, and longevity biotech, positioning them as part of an emerging health revolution. [^ygo8nj]
- [XPRIZE Foundation](https://www.xprize.org)[^ygo8nj] — Founded in the 1990s (with the Ansari XPRIZE announced in 1996), XPRIZE uses large-scale incentive competitions (over $600M in prizes launched) to spur breakthroughs in areas such as spaceflight, carbon removal, and education. [^n9hx8k] [^ygo8nj] [^3d4629]
- [Singularity University](https://www.su.org/experts/peter-h-diamandis)[^n8bmqm] [^87shnb] — Co‑founded with Ray Kurzweil, this organization functions as a learning and innovation community focused on "exponential technologies" and their application to global grand challenges. [^n8bmqm] [^87shnb]
- [MOONSHOTS podcast](https://www.diamandis.com/about)[^npg1p7] — A "top-ranked" podcast hosted by Diamandis, aimed at entrepreneurs and innovators, discussing moonshot thinking, exponential tech, and longevity. [^npg1p7]

## Why It Matters to Innovators

- He operationalizes "moonshot thinking" into repeatable mechanisms—like incentive prizes and [[Exponential Organizations]] design—showing how to translate audacious goals into structured competitions and ventures (e.g., XPRIZE). [^n8bmqm] [^ygo8nj] [^87shnb]
- His work popularizes a rigorously optimistic "abundance" lens on the future, encouraging innovators to see exponential technologies (AI, robotics, digital biology, space tech) as levers for tackling global grand challenges rather than threats alone. [^ygo8nj] [^npg1p7]
- Through XPRIZE and Singularity University, he has helped institutionalize prize-driven innovation and exponential-tech education, giving practitioners concrete models for open innovation, crowdsourcing, and [[Incentive Design]]. [^n8bmqm] [^ygo8nj] [^87shnb]
- His longevity and health-tech focus (Fountain Life, Life Force, Celularity, Vaxxinity) provides a roadmap for entrepreneurs interested in [[concepts/Longevity Tech]] and preventative, data-driven healthcare. [^ygo8nj] [^npg1p7] [[Longevity]]
- His books and talks embed mental models around harnessing exponential curves, leveraging communities, and aiming for 10x–100x impact, aligning closely with frameworks like [[concepts/10x Thinking]] and [[concepts/Massively Transformative Purpose]]. [^ygo8nj] [^npg1p7]
