# David Holz
![Headshot of David Holz speaking on a tech panel, labeled as founder of Midjourney](https://www.societyforscience.org/wp-content/uploads/sites/4/2026/01/NotableAlumni_headshot_DavidHolz.jpg?w=460)

*David Holz is a hands‑on technologist–founder whose work on gesture interfaces and generative AI has repeatedly opened up new ways for people to interact with computers.*[^lfh12u] [^73iup7]

David Holz is a **person**, an American entrepreneur and technologist best known as the founder and CEO of **[[Tooling/AI-Toolkit/Model Producers/Midjourney|Midjourney]]**, a leading generative AI image platform launched in 2021. [^lfh12u] [^73iup7] Before Midjourney, he co‑founded **Leap Motion**, a company pioneering hand‑tracking and motion‑based user interfaces that was later acquired by Ultrahaptics (now Ultraleap) in 2019. [^lfh12u] [^73iup7] He previously worked with [[NASA]]’s Langley Research Center and conducted neuroscience research at the Max Planck Institute before leaving a PhD program in applied mathematics at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill to start Leap Motion. [^73iup7] Innovators return to Holz as a reference point for how to turn deep technical insight in human–computer interaction into widely adopted products that reshape creative work and interfaces. [^lfh12u] [^73iup7]

# The People Behind It

Since the source is a **person**, this section is a brief bio of David Holz.

- **Origin and education**
  - David Holz is described as an **American** entrepreneur and technologist. [^lfh12u]  
  - He studied **applied mathematics** at the **University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill**, ultimately leaving his PhD program to co‑found Leap Motion. [^73iup7]  

- **Early research and technical background**
  - Before founding [[Leap Motion]], Holz **contracted for NASA’s Langley Research Center**, gaining experience in aerospace and computational work. [^73iup7] [^0wdpax]  
  - He also **conducted neuroscience research at the Max Planck Institute**, reflecting a cross‑disciplinary background spanning math, neuroscience, and computation. [^73iup7] [^i3yimg]  
  - He is an alumnus of the **2006 International Science and Engineering Fair**, indicating early involvement in high‑level scientific competition. [^73iup7]  

- **Entrepreneurial roles**
  - Holz co‑founded **Leap Motion** and served as its **CTO**, building “the world’s most advanced hand‑tracking technology” to enable natural, gesture‑based user interfaces. [^73iup7] Leap Motion created “a new paradigm of user interfaces based on hand motions and video capture.”[^lfh12u]  
  - Leap Motion was acquired by **[[Ultrahaptics]]** in 2019, and the combined entity is now known as **Ultraleap**. [^lfh12u] [^73iup7]  
  - In 2021, he founded **Midjourney**, which the Society for Science describes as “a generative artificial intelligence (AI)-powered platform that allows users to generate unique artwork…through short text prompts.”[^73iup7] He is its **Founder & CEO**. [^lfh12u] [^73iup7]  

- **Governance and community roles**
  - Holz joined the **Society for Science Board of Trustees** in 2026, indicating a governance role at a major science‑education nonprofit. [^73iup7]

# Catalog of Notable Works

As a **person**, Holz’s “works” are primarily companies, technologies, and public ideas rather than academic books.

- [Leap Motion: Product & Technology](https://www.leapmotion.com) — 2010s — Holz co‑founded Leap Motion, which developed an optical hand‑tracking system enabling users to control computers with natural hand movements in 3D space, often described as a “new paradigm of user interfaces.”[^lfh12u] [^73iup7] [^hd1a3h]  
- [Leap Motion acquisition by Ultrahaptics (Ultraleap)](https://www.ultraleap.com) — 2019 — This transaction combined Leap Motion’s hand‑tracking with Ultrahaptics’ haptic technologies, forming **Ultraleap**, now positioned as “the world’s most advanced hand‑tracking technology.”[^lfh12u] [^73iup7]  
- [Midjourney (AI image generator)](https://www.midjourney.com) — founded 2021 — A generative AI platform where users create images from text prompts via Discord and a web interface; often cited as “the most popular AI image generation app in the world.”[^lfh12u] [^73iup7] [^zenh71]  
- [David Holz on X (Twitter)](https://x.com/DavidSHolz) — ongoing — Holz regularly shares product changes, philosophy, and commentary on AI and creativity as “founder @midjourney, previously founded leap motion, before that was at nasa and max planck.”[^i3yimg]  
- “Lessons from David Holz” (summary/essay by Antoine Buteau) — 2023 — While not authored by Holz, this piece distills his public remarks into ideas such as Midjourney being “designed to unlock the creativity of ordinary people by giving them tools to make beautiful pictures just by describing them,” giving a concise window into his product philosophy. [^zenh71]  

Holz also appears as an author and cited researcher in technical literature associated with Leap Motion and computer vision; his Google Scholar profile lists work in **Computer Vision, Applied Math, Astronomy, and Physics**, anchored around Leap Motion. [^hd1a3h]

# Why It Matters to Innovators

- **Prototype‑driven interface innovation:** Holz’s trajectory from **hand‑tracking interfaces** (Leap Motion) to **text‑to‑image generative models** (Midjourney) offers a concrete pattern for how radically new interaction paradigms can move from research to consumer‑scale products—helpful when thinking about non‑obvious UI/UX frontiers, not just incremental improvements. [^lfh12u] [^73iup7]  
- **Democratizing creative capability:** His stated goal that Midjourney “unlock[s] the creativity of ordinary people by giving them tools to make beautiful pictures just by describing them” illustrates a strong [[concepts/Leverage the Machine]] mindset: push heavy computation into the background so non‑experts can perform expert‑level creative work. [^zenh71]  
- **Human‑centric framing of AI:** Holz consistently frames Midjourney as augmenting human imagination rather than replacing artists, offering a practical model for building **assistive AI** that aligns with [[concepts/Centaur Model]] approaches to human‑AI collaboration. [^zenh71]  
- **Cross‑disciplinary talent stack:** His path through applied math, neuroscience, NASA research, and startup founding exemplifies a [[concepts/Talent Stack]] approach—combining deep technical domains with product and design sensibilities to create defensible, hard‑to‑replicate innovations. [^73iup7] [^0wdpax] [^hd1a3h]  
- **Community‑first product development:** Midjourney’s initial deployment via Discord, with rapid iterative updates in public, is a live case study in **shipping early, iterating with a passionate user community**, and effectively doing large‑scale, real‑time [[concepts/Customer Discovery|Customer Discovery]] and feedback collection for frontier tech. [^lfh12u] [^zenh71]
