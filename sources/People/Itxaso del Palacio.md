### A Closer Look at Itxaso del Palacio’s Role and Impact

With a career rooted in engineering, entrepreneurship, and advanced study—a BSc and MSc in Engineering and a PhD in Economics and Venture Capital—Itxaso del Palacio brings a unique analytical rigor to venture capital. [^1w47mc] [^os0v3p] At Notion Capital, she has led investments in some of the most innovative SaaS companies across Europe, including GoCardless, CurrencyCloud, TradeShift, Unbabel, Yulife, Cledara, Onfido, and Beamery. [^1w47mc] [^7cfoh3]

Before joining Notion, Del Palacio held key roles at other major investment organizations, such as M12 (Microsoft Ventures) and Lepe Partners, focusing on early to growth-stage tech startups. [^1w47mc] [^os0v3p] [^jptm2q] Her approach is deeply product-led, favoring teams obsessed with building exceptional, user-centric solutions—a philosophy she credits with surfacing extra-resilient, high-growth companies, especially in turbulent times. [^jptm2q] [^7cfoh3]

In addition to her investment work, del Palacio is a respected educator. She has taught entrepreneurship at leading institutions including University College London (UCL) and Imperial College, contributing to the development of future startup founders and investors. [^1w47mc] [^os0v3p] [^kuz37z]
