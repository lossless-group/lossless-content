# Ken Griffin (Hedge Fund founder)

- ![Kenneth C. Griffin official Citadel leadership headshot](https://www.citadel.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/10/Ken_Griffin-2.jpg)

_*Ken Griffin is the founder of Citadel and Citadel Securities, and a central figure in modern hedge-fund and market-making infrastructure.*_[^339ox9]

Ken Griffin is a **person**: an American hedge fund manager, investor, and entrepreneur who founded Citadel in 1990 and Citadel Securities in 2002. [^339ox9] [^ipz7ko] Citadel describes him as Founder and CEO of Citadel and Founder and Non-Executive Chairman of Citadel Securities, and says he graduated from Harvard College in 1989 before starting Citadel a year later. [^339ox9] Innovators return to Griffin because his career sits at the intersection of capital allocation, market structure, and scale—useful for understanding how quantitative advantage is built and defended. [^339ox9] [^6vkh04]

# The People Behind It

- Griffin graduated from Harvard College in 1989 with an A.B. in Economics with Honors, according to Citadel. [^339ox9]
- Citadel says he founded Citadel in 1990, a year after graduating, and established Citadel Securities in 2002 with his partners. [^339ox9]
- Citadel identifies him as Founder and CEO of Citadel and Founder and Non-Executive Chairman of Citadel Securities. [^339ox9]
- Stanford GSB describes him as the founder and CEO of Citadel and notes the firm as “the most profitable hedge fund in history,” framing his role as both operator and capital allocator. [^6vkh04]
