An infographic is a visual representation of information or data. It combines elements like charts, graphs, images, and minimal text to convey complex information quickly and clearly. Infographics are designed to make data more accessible, understandable, and engaging. They're widely used in fields such as business, education, healthcare, and marketing for communicating statistics, processes, or concepts in a visually appealing way.
