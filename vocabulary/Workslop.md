# **Workslop: Navigating the New Workplace Epidemic of AI-Generated Busywork**

### Understanding Workslop

Workslop refers explicitly to documents, memos, reports, slide decks, code, or presentations created using AI tools that may seem “good enough” at first glance, yet do not meaningfully advance the task at hand.
