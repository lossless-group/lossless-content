# Deloitte

![Deloitte logo on a white background](https://assets.bizclikmedia.net/1800/959403848f2b152096f544687dd293e6:abff61d2113285684303bc6325f11a76/deloitte-timeline.jpg)

_One of the “Big Four” professional-services networks, Deloitte combines audit, consulting, tax, and advisory practices into a global platform serving many of the world’s largest organizations._[^1cfsh3] [^9nqaro]

Deloitte is a global professional-services firm providing “industry-leading audit, consulting, tax and advisory services to many of the world's most admired brands.”[^9nqaro] It operates as a network of member firms under the umbrella of Deloitte Touche Tohmatsu Limited (DTTL), a UK private company limited by guarantee, with significant presence in markets including the US, UK, and across EMEA, Asia-Pacific, and the Americas. [^1cfsh3] [^qu0efp] [^hzbdi5] [^txj12k] The network traces its roots to 1845 and today is headquartered at the global level in London while serving clients worldwide across sectors such as consumer, financial services, government and public services, and technology, media & telecommunications. [^1cfsh3] [^9nqaro] Consultants track Deloitte because it is both a competitor and collaborator on complex transformations and a bellwether for trends in corporate and public-sector advisory work. [^1cfsh3] [^9nqaro]

## Mission and Identity

> “Deloitte provides industry-leading audit, consulting, tax and advisory services to many of the world's most admired brands, including nearly 90% of the Fortune 500® and more than 7,000 private companies.”[^9nqaro]

Deloitte positions itself as a trusted advisor that combines multidisciplinary expertise and global reach to help clients address complex challenges in areas such as strategy, risk, technology, and regulatory compliance. [^1cfsh3] [^9nqaro] [^qu0efp] It emphasizes working across industries—from consumer and financial services to government and technology, media & telecommunications—to deliver outcomes enabled by capabilities in areas like AI & engineering, cyber, cloud, and finance transformation. [^1cfsh3] [^qu0efp] Its public messaging highlights investment in its people and diversity, and the use of “the latest technologies, talent and capabilities from all over the world to bring the best of Deloitte and deliver ongoing outcomes” across domains such as tax compliance, cyber, financial crime, and cloud and application management. [^qu0efp]

- **Stated values / principles (representative published themes):** Deloitte (including its Operate Services and Global entities) emphasizes harnessing global talent and technology to deliver “ongoing outcomes,” a focus on multidisciplinary teaming, and commitments to inclusion and supporting diverse career paths, as reflected in materials from Deloitte Global and people-focused stories. [^qu0efp] [^hzbdi5] [^5zno0y]

## What They Do

Deloitte generates value by delivering professional services—principally audit & assurance, consulting, tax & legal, and risk / financial advisory—to corporate, public-sector, and nonprofit clients, typically through project-based and ongoing advisory engagements. [^1cfsh3] [^9nqaro] [^qu0efp] It increasingly delivers managed services and “Operate” offerings that provide ongoing, technology-enabled operations support in areas such as cyber, tax compliance, and cloud, alongside strategic consulting and implementation. [^qu0efp] [^hzbdi5] Revenue is primarily derived from fees for these services across its global network of member firms. [^1cfsh3] [^9nqaro]

**Main offerings and service lines**

- **Audit & Assurance:** Independent audit and assurance services for listed and private companies, forming one of Deloitte’s core traditional businesses. [^1cfsh3] [^9nqaro]
- **Consulting:** Strategy, operations, human capital, and technology consulting, including specialized practices in AI & engineering, finance transformation, and other digital and transformation domains. [^1cfsh3] [^wn54f2]
- **Tax & Legal:** Corporate, international, and indirect tax services, alongside legal offerings in jurisdictions where permitted, including support for tax compliance and planning. [^1cfsh3] [^qu0efp]
- **Risk & Advisory:** Advisory services spanning risk management, regulatory, financial crime, and related areas, including cyber risk and internal controls. [^1cfsh3] [^qu0efp]
- **Deloitte Digital:** A digital consultancy that “provides online and mobile strategy, design and development,” leveraging resources from the broader business and technology consultancy. [^n6mcld]
- **Operate / Managed Services:** Deloitte Operate Services delivers ongoing, technology-enabled operations in “tax compliance, cyber, financial crime, cloud and application management,” going beyond traditional business process outsourcing. [^qu0efp]
- **Sector-focused practices:** Dedicated teams serving sectors such as Consumer, Financial Services, Government & Public Services, and Technology, Media & Telecommunications. [^1cfsh3] [^9nqaro]

## History and Origin Story

Deloitte originated in 1845 when William Welch Deloitte opened his accounting office in London, establishing one of the earliest professional accounting practices that would evolve into today’s global network. [^1cfsh3] Over time, Deloitte merged with other firms and expanded internationally, eventually forming Deloitte Touche Tohmatsu Limited (DTTL) as a coordinating entity for member firms, and diversifying from audit into consulting, tax, and a broad set of advisory and digital services. [^1cfsh3] [^9nqaro] [^qu0efp] Significant inflection points have included the expansion into consulting, the creation of Deloitte Digital as a branded digital consultancy, the development of Operate / managed services offerings, and the firm’s role in global rankings and recognition programs like the US Best Managed Companies program. [^n6mcld] [^9nqaro] [^qu0efp]

**Key dated milestones**

- **1845:** Deloitte is founded; StartupHub.ai notes that “Deloitte was founded in 1845,” with London as its headquarters, marking its origin as an accounting firm. [^1cfsh3]
- **Late 20th century (DTTL formation):** Deloitte NSE LLP (and other regional entities) are described as “member firms of Deloitte Touche Tohmatsu Limited, a UK private company limited by guarantee,” indicating the establishment of the DTTL global network structure during the late 20th century; the exact year is not given in the current results but the structure is confirmed. [^qu0efp]
- **Creation of Deloitte Digital (early 2010s):** Deloitte Digital appears as a distinct entity “based out of New York, NY,” providing “online and mobile strategy, design and development,” representing Deloitte’s formal push into digital consultancy under a separate brand. [^n6mcld]
- **Development of Operate Services (by mid‑2020s):** The “People of Operate Services” page describes Deloitte Operate as a global offering delivering outcomes in tax compliance, cyber, financial crime, and cloud and application management, evidencing a strategic move into long-term, managed services. [^qu0efp]
- **2026:** Deloitte runs or co-runs the “US Best Managed Companies” program, with a 2026 honorees list, reflecting its role in recognition programs and its ongoing prominence in the US market. [^9nqaro]

## Impact

**Impact on society**

- Deloitte’s Gen Z and Millennial survey, now in its 15th year and based on more than 22,500 respondents in 44 countries, is widely cited by media and organizations to understand youth perspectives on work, climate, and social issues, influencing public debates and corporate policies. [^txj12k]
- By advising “many of the world's most admired brands, including nearly 90% of the Fortune 500® and more than 7,000 private companies,” Deloitte shapes corporate governance, reporting, and transformation efforts at large scale, with indirect effects on employees, consumers, and communities. [^9nqaro]

**Impact on innovation**

- Deloitte Digital’s focus on “online and mobile strategy, design and development,” combined with its consulting heritage, has helped popularize integrated digital transformation programs that blend strategy, design, and implementation across industries. [^n6mcld] [^9nqaro]
- Deloitte’s Operate Services model, which delivers ongoing outcomes in areas such as cyber, financial crime, and cloud/application management, contributes to the trend toward managed and “as-a-service” operations in professional services, influencing how organizations source and manage critical functions. [^qu0efp]

**Impact on its industry or domain**

- As one of the largest global professional-services networks, with over 500,000 people and multi-billion-dollar revenue segments like Deloitte Digital alone, Deloitte sets competitive benchmarks for service breadth, global coverage, and integrated offerings that other consultancies and accounting firms respond to. [^1cfsh3] [^n6mcld]
- Programs like the US Best Managed Companies recognition initiative reinforce Deloitte’s role as a standard-setter for management quality among private companies, influencing how firms evaluate governance, strategy, and financial performance. [^9nqaro]

**Historical significance**

- Founded in 1845 and evolving into a global network structure (DTTL), Deloitte is part of the historical core of the modern accounting and professional-services industry, contributing to the development of global audit practices and the evolution from pure accounting to multi-line advisory firms.
