# Akamai

![Akamai logo and hero banner from akamai.com homepage highlighting “cybersecurity and cloud computing company that powers and protects life online.”](https://image.slidesharecdn.com/akamaicompanyprofile-140922092651-phpapp02/85/Akamai-company-profile-2-320.jpg)

_An established cloud and security infrastructure provider, Akamai combines a globally distributed edge platform with cybersecurity services to secure and accelerate digital experiences for large enterprises and digital-native companies._

Akamai is a **for-profit technology company** best known as a global leader in content delivery networks (CDNs), cloud security, and edge/cloud computing services. [^6cobq6] [^sp0crq] It describes itself as “the cybersecurity and cloud computing company that powers and protects life online.”[^6cobq6] [^2mguo2] Akamai Technologies, Inc. was founded in 1998 and is headquartered in Cambridge, Massachusetts, with a global infrastructure footprint spanning more than 130 countries. [^sp0crq] Consultants track Akamai because it is a bellwether for edge-native architectures, application security, and the evolution of CDNs into full cloud platforms.

## What They Do

Akamai operates a global edge and cloud platform that provides content delivery, application and API security, zero trust access, and compute services close to users. [^6cobq6] [^sp0crq] [^bd0tma] It generates revenue by selling these infrastructure and security services to enterprises, media and entertainment providers, SaaS companies, and other organizations that need high‑performance, secure online experiences. [^6cobq6] [^sp0crq] [^2mguo2]

- **Content delivery & media services:** CDN and streaming delivery to distribute web content, software, and video globally, historically Akamai’s core market where it is described as “the global leader in the content delivery network market.”[^sp0crq]

- **Application & API security:** Web application firewalls, bot management, DDoS protection, and other application security services delivered from its edge platform, marketed as “market‑leading security solutions” with “superior threat intelligence.”[^6cobq6] [^2mguo2]

- **Zero trust & access solutions:** Secure access to applications and services using identity-aware, zero trust approaches; details are implied in Akamai’s positioning as a cybersecurity company but not elaborated in the retrieved snippets. [^6cobq6] [^2mguo2]

- **Edge compute and serverless (EdgeWorkers, Akamai Functions):** EdgeWorkers and Akamai Functions enable customers to run code at the edge; for example, a streaming video customer used EdgeWorkers to implement cryptographically signed tokens (CATs) to combat media piracy at scale, with the platform handling “trillions of invocations.”[^bd0tma]

- **Anti‑piracy and media protection:** Token-based access control and other measures to prevent unauthorized streaming, “allow[ing] validation of tokens at extreme volumes” for live and on‑demand video. [^bd0tma]

- **Threat intelligence and security operations:** Akamai references “superior threat intelligence” as a differentiator, implying continuous monitoring and analysis of global traffic flows to update defenses. [^6cobq6] [^2mguo2]

- **Customer solutions / consulting around performance and security:** Customer stories (e.g., Netstock) show Akamai working with clients to protect their environments and support online business operations as part of broader solution engagements. [^6cobq6]

## History and Origin Story

Akamai Technologies, Inc. was founded in 1998 in the United States, originally focused on solving the problem of internet congestion and slow web content delivery by distributing content across a wide network of servers. [^sp0crq] Over time, it evolved from a pure CDN provider into a broader platform company encompassing security, edge compute, and cloud services, culminating in its current positioning as a cybersecurity and cloud computing provider with a presence in more than 130 countries. [^6cobq6] [^sp0crq] [^2mguo2]

- **1998 – Founding:** Akamai Technologies, Inc. is founded in the United States to address web content delivery and internet performance challenges. [^sp0crq]

- **2000s – CDN leadership:** Akamai becomes recognized as “the global leader in the content delivery network market,” according to S&P Global Ratings. [^sp0crq]

- **2020s – Expansion into cybersecurity and cloud computing:** Akamai explicitly frames itself as “the cybersecurity and cloud computing company that powers and protects life online,” highlighting a strategic focus beyond CDN into security and cloud. [^6cobq6] [^2mguo2]

- **By mid‑2020s – Global footprint:** Its Akamai Connected Cloud platform grows to have “a presence in more than 130 countries,” underscoring its global infrastructure expansion. [^sp0crq]

## Impact

- **Impact on society:**
  - By enabling secure, high‑performance delivery of web and streaming content, Akamai helps “power and protect life online” for billions of people “billions of times a day,” underpinning everyday digital experiences from media consumption to SaaS tools. [^6cobq6] [^2mguo2]
  - Its anti‑piracy and media protection solutions help content providers reduce economic losses from piracy, with one streaming customer’s deployment of CATs designed to address large-scale revenue leakage tied to unauthorized streaming. [^bd0tma]

- **Impact on innovation:**
  - Akamai has been a key driver in the evolution of the **content delivery network** model, with S&P calling it “the global leader” in the CDN market, thereby influencing how web performance and scalability are engineered across the internet. [^sp0crq]
  - Its EdgeWorkers and Akamai Functions offerings exemplify the shift toward **edge computing**, enabling application logic to run at the network edge and supporting new architectures for security and content protection at massive scale. [^bd0tma]

- **Impact on its industry or domain:**
  - Akamai’s scale and leadership in CDN services have set performance and reliability benchmarks that other content delivery and edge platforms must match, influencing pricing, SLAs, and architectural norms in the CDN and edge markets. [^sp0crq]
  - Its push into cybersecurity and cloud computing positions it as a hybrid of CDN and cloud‑security provider, encouraging incumbents and new entrants to converge content delivery, edge compute, and security services into integrated platforms. [^6cobq6] [^sp0crq] [^2mguo2]

- **Historical significance:**
  - As an early and enduring leader in CDN technology since the late 1990s, Akamai has played a significant role in enabling the modern, media‑rich web and large‑scale streaming, helping to make high‑bandwidth, low‑latency content delivery a standard expectation for internet users. [^sp0crq]
