# Global Skill Development Council

![Global Skill Development Council (GSDC) logo from gsdcouncil.org](https://d1011j0lbv5k1u.cloudfront.net/assets/store-614024/614024-logo-1763098569.png)

*An independent, vendor‑neutral international certification body focused on digital transformation, emerging technologies, and professional skills.*

Global Skill Development Council (GSDC) is an independent, vendor‑neutral international credentialing and certification organization that brands itself as “your gateway to digital transformation.”[^vfbl0l][^8g2rx9] It offers more than 100 globally accredited certification programs across domains such as generative AI, HR and learning & development, project management, ISO and quality management, and information security, serving individual professionals and enterprises worldwide.[^vfbl0l] GSDC highlights offices in Switzerland, the United States, and Singapore, reflecting a global operational footprint.[^vfbl0l][^5ij3y9] Consultants track GSDC as a fast‑moving certification provider shaping skill benchmarks in emerging technology and transformation‑related roles.[^vfbl0l][^8g2rx9]

## Mission and Identity

> “We are an independent vendor‑neutral ISOC 1024 accredited certification body committed to helping individuals and organizations stay future ready in a rapidly evolving world.”[^vfbl0l]

GSDC positions itself as “your gateway to digital transformation,” emphasizing future‑readiness for both individuals and organizations through globally accredited certifications in emerging and in‑demand domains.[^vfbl0l] It describes itself as “vendor agnostic, vendor neutral, completely independent” and highlights recognition by publications such as Forbes, TechTarget, and People Managing People, along with input from “100+ global advisory board members” in crafting its programs.[^vfbl0l]

- **Stated values / principles:** GSDC’s messaging emphasizes vendor‑neutrality and independence, global accessibility, future‑readiness (“stay future ready”), and alignment with industry needs via a large global advisory board and ISOC 1024 / ABICB accreditations.[^vfbl0l][^8g2rx9]

## What They Do

GSDC develops and administers professional certification programs, exams, and related learning offerings across a broad portfolio of emerging technologies and business disciplines.[^vfbl0l][^8g2rx9] It generates value by validating skills for individual professionals and by providing organizations with standardized, externally accredited benchmarks for capability in areas like digital transformation, AI, HR, and compliance.[^vfbl0l][^5ij3y9][^8g2rx9]

Key offerings and activities:

- Certification programs in “generative AI and emerging technologies,” including agentic AI and other digital‑transformation‑oriented topics.[^vfbl0l]
- HR and Learning & Development certifications such as “Certified Learning and Development Professional,” featured in both badges and webinars.[^h6m8bk][^vfbl0l]
- Project management and Agile/Scrum certifications, including roles linked to PMO and agile practices.[^vfbl0l]
- [[organizations/ISO|ISO]] and quality management certifications, including ISO lead auditor and implementer pathways (e.g., ISO 18404:2015 Lead Auditor).[^vfbl0l][^8g2rx9]
- Information security and cybersecurity certifications aligned with standards and risk‑management frameworks (e.g., ISO 31000‑related content).[^vfbl0l][^yjd3rl]
- Digital transformation certifications that address organizational change, leadership, and adoption challenges, positioned to reduce transformation failure rates.[^vfbl0l]
- Membership and community benefits, with webinars, lifelong access to interactive learning resources, and advisory‑board‑driven content for ongoing professional development.[^vfbl0l]
- Collaboration with partners and training providers (e.g., Vision Africa AI) that deliver courses preparing candidates for GSDC certifications.[^8g2rx9]

## History and Origin Story

GSDC describes itself as having reached “10 years” and cites certification of over 100,000 professionals in that period, implying a founding around the mid‑2010s, but it does not publicly provide a precise founding date, founder names, or a detailed origin narrative.[^vfbl0l] Its trajectory appears to follow the rise of digital transformation and emerging technology certifications, expanding from initial offerings into a portfolio of 100+ programs across technology, HR, quality, and security domains.[^vfbl0l][^8g2rx9]

Notable timeline points (where available):

## Impact

- **Impact on society**
  - By certifying “more than 100,000 professionals across Europe, North America, Asia, and Africa,” GSDC has contributed to upskilling a sizable global workforce in areas such as digital transformation, AI, and quality management.[^vfbl0l]

  - Its broad coverage—from AI and digital transformation to ISO standards and L&D—creates cross‑functional certification pathways that encourage integrated skill profiles across technology, HR, and governance.[^vfbl0l][^8g2rx9]

- **Historical significance**
