### Main Content

[^hh0z4u] [^n3ed2g] It pursues its mission through three pillars: THINK (research and thought leadership), XCHANGE (convening summits and connecting a global community), and ACT (direct impact investments in promising startups and technologies). [^hh0z4u] [^n3ed2g]

The practical benefits of the FII Institute’s model include:
- **Accelerating innovation** by channeling investment to high-impact startups. [^hh0z4u]
- **Fostering global collaboration** through cross-sector summits and a membership community of change-makers.
