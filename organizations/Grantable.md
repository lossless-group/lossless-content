# What They Do

Grantable provides a **web-based grant-writing workspace** that uses large language models to help users draft, refine, and organize grant application content, while tracking requirements and deadlines in a single place. The platform is designed to integrate existing organization materials (e.g., prior proposals, background docs) and turn them into tailored responses for specific application questions, with tools for collaboration and version control.

Key offerings and product capabilities:

- **AI‑assisted drafting:** Uses large language models to “draft responses based on your existing materials” and help “write stronger applications in less time,” reducing time spent starting from scratch.

- **Grant workspace:** A central workspace for each grant where users can break down requirements, track tasks, and store drafts and supporting documents.

- **Content library / knowledge base:** Ability to upload and reuse past proposals and organizational materials so the AI can pull relevant language and data into new applications.

- **Collaboration tools:** Support for teams to work together on applications, including shared workspaces and organized sections for different contributors.

- **Guided structure and prompts:** Pre-structured sections and prompts that reflect common grant application formats, helping users respond comprehensively and avoid missing required information.

- **Time-saving automation:** Marketing language emphasizes that Grantable helps users “stop reinventing the wheel” and rapidly adapt existing content to new opportunities.

- **Support and onboarding content:** Site content includes explanatory pages and examples on how to use AI safely and effectively in grant-writing workflows.
