# Goldman Sachs

![Goldman Sachs corporate logo or New York headquarters exterior](https://fourweekmba.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/11/organizational-structures.png)

- _Goldman Sachs is a global financial-services firm that packages investment banking, markets, asset management, and wealth management for large institutions, corporations, and high-net-worth clients._[^r2tgs3] [^9gvxcq]
- It was founded in 1869 and is headquartered at 200 West Street in New York City, with a global footprint across the Americas, Europe, and Asia. [^r2tgs3] [^9gvxcq]
- Consultants track Goldman Sachs because it sits at the center of capital formation, market liquidity, and institutional wealth flows, so its strategy is often an early signal of where finance is heading. [^r2tgs3] [^9gvxcq]

## Identity and Form

- **Type:** This organization is a **for-profit company**. [^r2tgs3] [^9gvxcq]
- **Legal form and jurisdiction:** It is publicly held through The Goldman Sachs Group, Inc., and its futures commission merchant disclosure states that Goldman Sachs & Co. LLC is a subsidiary of The Goldman Sachs Group, Inc. [^r2tgs3]
- **Headquarters and presence:** Its principal office is in New York City, and its business is described as worldwide and full-service. [^r2tgs3] [^9gvxcq]
- **Size:** No recent verifiable headcount figure was returned in the search results. [^r2tgs3] [^9gvxcq]
- **Where it lives online:** Primary [Homepage](https://www.goldmansachs.com); secondary surface: its disclosures and product pages on the Goldman Sachs domain, including the CFTC FCM disclosure document and asset-management fund pages. [^r2tgs3] [^9gvxcq] [^d6fmgt]

## Mission and Identity

> “a worldwide, full-service investment banking, broker-dealer, asset management and financial services organization.”[^9gvxcq]

Goldman Sachs positions itself as a broad financial intermediary serving institutional, commercial, and retail clients through banking, markets, custody, and asset-management capabilities. [^r2tgs3] [^9gvxcq] Its own disclosures emphasize multinational reach, client breadth, and regulated market participation, including futures, swaps, and broker-dealer activity. [^r2tgs3] The firm’s self-image is that of an infrastructure layer for global capital markets rather than a single-product bank. [^r2tgs3] [^9gvxcq]

- **Core tenets:** No explicit company-values page was returned in the search results. [^r2tgs3] [^9gvxcq]

## What They Do

Goldman Sachs runs a diversified financial-services business that arranges financing, trades financial instruments, manages assets, and provides custody and brokerage services. [^r2tgs3] [^9gvxcq] The firm earns value by intermediating capital, taking market and credit risk where permitted, and charging fees or spreads across banking, trading, and asset-management activity. [^r2tgs3] [^9gvxcq]

- Investment banking and advisory services. [^9gvxcq]
- Markets and trading across financial, agricultural, energy, metals, securities futures, and swaps. [^r2tgs3]
- Asset management through Goldman Sachs Asset Management. [^0412ii] [^d6fmgt]
- Custody solutions for institutional clients. [^9gvxcq]
- Futures commission merchant services under U.S. CFTC rules. [^r2tgs3]
- Broker-dealer services. [^r2tgs3]
- International client coverage across Europe, Asia, and Latin America. [^r2tgs3]

## History and Origin Story

Goldman Sachs began in 1869 and has evolved into a full-service global financial institution with regulated broker-dealer, FCM, swap-dealer, asset-management, and custody businesses. [^r2tgs3] [^9gvxcq] The available search results do not provide a richer founder-by-founder origin narrative, so the account here is limited to what is directly supported. [^r2tgs3] [^9gvxcq]

- **1869** — Founded. [^r2tgs3] [^9gvxcq]
- **Current era** — Described in disclosures as a worldwide, full-service investment banking, broker-dealer, asset management and financial services organization. [^9gvxcq]
- **Current era** — Operates as a registered U.S. broker-dealer, futures commission merchant, and swap dealer through GS&Co. [^r2tgs3]
- **Current era** — Runs Goldman Sachs Asset Management as the external manager of GSBD, according to Fitch. [^0412ii]

- **Impact on society** — Goldman Sachs PAC contributions were split almost evenly between Democrats and Republicans in 2023–2024, showing the firm’s political footprint in U.S. finance policy debates. [^e3g0zp]
