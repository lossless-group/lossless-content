# Value Proposition & Features

Console is an **AI-native IT service management and access management platform** that helps IT teams “get to Inbox Zero” by auto-resolving more than 50% of support requests directly in Slack and Microsoft Teams using AI agents trained on how the company works. [^p1mgrf] Console centralizes **access requests, approvals, provisioning, and user access reviews** so IT can enforce least-privilege policies while shifting focus from ticket handling to strategic work. [^4y204o]

Console’s core value is using AI that “knows your people, your tools, and your systems better than any other tool in your stack” to autonomously handle routine IT support and access workflows. [^4y204o] It turns existing access matrices and policies into executable workflows, automatically granting, revoking, and reviewing access, and logging everything end-to-end to reduce risk and audit effort. [^4y204o]

**Key features (priority order):**

- **AI agents for IT support in Slack and Teams** that auto-resolve ~50%+ of support requests, helping IT teams reach “Inbox Zero” in their ticket queues. [^p1mgrf]
- **AI-native access request and approval hub** where access is “requested, approved, and provisioned” with automated least-privilege enforcement and approval collection. [^4y204o]
- **Automated access provisioning and revocation** via SCIM, APIs, or workflows, with scheduled automatic removal when access expires. [^4y204o]
- **Import and operationalize access matrices** by uploading CSV exports that Console turns into “live, enforceable access policies.”[^4y204o]
- **AI-powered User Access Reviews** that pull entitlements for each app, consolidate user histories, and streamline review decisions in one place. [^4y204o]
- **Risk prevention via role- and team-based policies** that restrict sensitive systems to approved roles so “risky requests never reach production.”[^4y204o]
- **Centralized access history for every user** so IT can see everything a user has been given access to and run accurate reviews without other tools. [^4y204o]
- **Productivity “force multiplier” for IT teams**, described by customers as “the force multiplier that allows 5 people to do the work of 50” and “already solving 75% of requests autonomously.”[^4y204o]

# Competitive Landscape

## Who it's for, who it's not for

Console is for **enterprise and mid-market IT teams** that manage high volumes of internal support requests and access changes, and that already rely heavily on Slack or Microsoft Teams for employee communication and ticket intake. [^p1mgrf] It is particularly suited to organizations that care about least-privilege access, auditability, and using AI to automate IT service management workflows like password resets, onboarding/offboarding access, and access reviews. [^4y204o] [^2m57be]

Console is less suitable for **very small organizations** with low IT ticket volume or minimal access complexity, where the overhead of an AI-native ITSM and access management platform may outweigh the benefits. [^4y204o] [^p1mgrf] It is also not aimed at consumer-facing support use cases or non-IT support domains, as its workflows and Gartner categorization focus specifically on IT service management and internal access governance. [^2m57be]

## Viable Alternatives

- **ServiceNow** – A leading ITSM platform with extensive automation and workflow capabilities, widely used for IT support and access-related processes at large enterprises. [^2m57be]
- **Jira Service Management (Atlassian)** – An IT service desk and support tool that integrates with collaboration platforms and can manage IT workflows, though not natively AI-first for Slack/Teams tickets. [^2m57be]
- **Zendesk** – A customer and employee support platform that can handle IT tickets but is more general-purpose than Console’s ITSM and IAM focus. [^2m57be]
- **Okta** – A major identity and access management provider focused on SSO, lifecycle management, and access governance, overlapping with Console’s access management capabilities but less focused on Slack/Teams support flows. [^4y204o] [^2m57be]
- **Microsoft Entra / Azure AD-based IAM solutions** – Enterprise IAM services that manage access, roles, and policies, offering an alternative approach to access governance and reviews. [^4y204o] [^2m57be]
