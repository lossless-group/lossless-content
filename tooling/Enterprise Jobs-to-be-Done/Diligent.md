# Value Proposition & Features

Diligent is an AI-led **governance, risk, and compliance (GRC) SaaS** platform that helps organizations manage board work, entity compliance, risk, audit, ESG, and related workflows in one system. [^8h88q6] [^ag0coq] Its positioning emphasizes board productivity and decision support, with Diligent describing benefits such as summarizing and comparing materials, building agendas, drafting disclosures, and analyzing trends. [^d6cj02] [^k1idkl]

- **Board management:** Diligent Boards supports board and committee materials, collaboration, and document management, and is described as part of the broader Diligent AI and Diligent One Platform stack. [^w03z3e] [^d6cj02]
- **Entity management:** The Entities product focuses on secure data and document storage, categorization, reporting, and management of company data in a secure environment. [^ag0coq]
- **Integrated platform:** Diligent One connects governance, risk, compliance, audit, entities, and ESG so teams can work from shared data and workflows. [^d6cj02]
- **AI-assisted workflow:** Diligent’s own marketing says the platform uses AI to streamline board activities, including summarization, comparison, agenda building, and disclosure drafting. [^k1idkl]
- **Governance and compliance breadth:** Public references show Diligent positioning itself across board management, governance, risk, compliance, legal entity compliance, and related oversight use cases. [^8h88q6] [^ag0coq] [^k1idkl]

# Competitive Landscape

## Who it's for, who it's not for

Diligent appears aimed at **board members, corporate secretaries, legal ops leaders, general counsel, compliance teams, audit teams, and governance leaders** who need shared workflows and controlled access to board and entity information. [^8h88q6] [^ag0coq] [^k1idkl] Its published materials point to organizations that want one platform for board management, entity compliance, and broader GRC coordination. [^d6cj02] [^k1idkl]

It is not primarily positioned for small teams that only need simple meeting scheduling or lightweight document sharing, because the public materials emphasize enterprise governance, compliance, and multi-workflow coordination. [^ag0coq] [^d6cj02] It is also a poor fit for buyers seeking a standalone point tool rather than a platform spanning boards, entities, risk, audit, and ESG. [^d6cj02]
